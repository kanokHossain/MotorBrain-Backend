# Feature Ideas & Brainstorming

These are raw feature ideas not yet planned or prioritized.

---

## Vehicle Management

- Vehicle document storage (registration, insurance, warranty)
- Insurance renewal reminders
- Roadworthy test / MOT reminders
- Vehicle comparison by cost efficiency

## Maintenance

- QR code scan for spare parts (auto-fill brand/part info)
- Photo recognition for service receipts (OCR)
- Scheduled maintenance plans from manufacturer specs
- Mechanic notes and labour cost tracking

## Mileage & Fuel

- Google Maps integration to auto-calculate trip distance
- Automatic fuel refill detection via bank transaction import
- Fuel price alerts (notify when nearby station has cheaper fuel)
- Carbon footprint tracker based on fuel consumption

## AI / Predictive

- Predict next major service cost based on vehicle age and usage
- Alert when fuel efficiency drops significantly (engine issue signal)
- Compare user's vehicle health against similar vehicles in database
- Seasonal maintenance recommendations

## Social / Community

- Share vehicle timeline publicly (for car enthusiasts)
- Mechanic ratings and reviews
- Community tips per vehicle model

## Business Features

- Fleet management dashboard
- Multi-tenant support for garages and service centers
- API access for third-party integrations

## Monetization

- One-time purchase option (lifetime plan)
- Family plan (multiple users under one subscription)
- Affiliate revenue from parts and service bookings
